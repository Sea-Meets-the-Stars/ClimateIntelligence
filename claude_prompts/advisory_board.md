# Advisory Board

## Goals

This prompt doc will on documents related to the advisory board of Climate Intelligence and its members.

## Context

See your context file `context/claudes_context.md` for the current context.

The advisory board of Climate Intelligence is a group of individuals who provide advice on the climate crisis and its solutions.  It is intended to represent a broad cross-section of the population in the United States, although CI will hope to impact persons outside the U.S.  

The board will be advisory.  If CI leadership does not follow the advice of the board, the board will be free to publish its findings and recommendations to the public and CI leadership will be required to acknowledge them and respond in writing.

The Board will elect its own Chair and members.
CI leadership will not have veto power on membership but can disband the entire board.

The Board will form with two members: Richard Nolthenius and Andrew Bojko.
