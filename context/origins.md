# Origins

This file will describe the origins of the Climate Intelligence blog.

## Early

JXP read James Hansen's book "Storms of My Grandchildren" and was inspired to write a blog about climate change.  At that time, he started a mini-blog for his Princeton college roomate (Andrw Bojko) who was quite skeptical of climate change.  


## More recent

On New Years Day 2024, JXP decided to re-engage with climate change.  His efforts at that time were to do a bit more reading and create a few slide decks to summarize the state of affairs.  He was heavily inspired by Tom Murphy's book "Energy and Human Ambitions on a Finite Planet".
