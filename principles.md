# Guiding Principles

The following are the guiding principles for the Climate Intelligence blog.

1. Let fact trump opinion, but recognize all human measurements bear uncertainty (and bias!)
2. Welcome all viewpoints, but do not accept them equally.
3. All life on Earth is relevant and human life need not be prioritized
4. Leave religion (and political viewpoints) out of this, whenever possible.
5. Statistics must be respected. A low-probability event really does have a low probability of occurring.
6. But mathematics trumps all. An equal sign means equals. No if, ands or buts.
